TOP SECRET

Deep beneath the surface of Europa, hidden in a glacial cavern warmed by alien technology, lies the Arc-Catalyst—an ancient weapon rumored to predate even the first stars. Encased in obsidian armor and humming with quantum resonance, it doesn't just destroy matter—it erases the probability of it ever existing. Earth’s black ops divisions call it "The Ghost Pulse," though no operative who's seen it in action has returned with a clear memory.

Recovered during a silent skirmish with a rogue Martian outpost, the Arc-Catalyst emits pulses that render conventional physics meaningless in a radius of 200 meters. Bullets curve, time stutters, and language collapses into raw emotion. Some scientists believe it’s powered by sentient dark matter; others insist it's a prison, not a weapon—meant to keep something far worse inside.

Currently housed in a zero-contact stasis vault at Site Theta-12, the weapon is guarded by AI constructs with no human oversight. Only one person holds the access code: a linguist who accidentally activated the device by speaking an extinct Sumerian dialect during a routine analysis. She hasn’t spoken since.
